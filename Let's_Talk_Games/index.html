Our website's games are categorized into three categories. BRAIN TEASER is the first category. Example, a puzzle that requires thought to solve is referred to as a brainteaser. It frequently calls for unconventional thinking while keeping the constraints in mind, and it occasionally also calls for lateral thinking. Riddles and logic puzzles fall under the category of "brain teasers."

Racing games make up the second section. The goal of this game is to avoid the obstacles on the tracks and make it to the finish line as quickly as you can while surviving as long as you can. We use a currently available car racing game, which is depicted in the following image, to demonstrate what we ultimately want to achieve.

SPORTS GAMES make up the third section. Some of the most well-known video games available right now are sports games. A "traditional" or "real world" sport, such as football, basketball, American football, tennis, etc., is attempted to be simulated in these video games.
